Microwaved Quesadilla

1–2 servings • 1–2 minutes

Ingredients
  • 2 tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Optional fillings (beans, pre-cooked chicken, salsa)
Instructions
  1. Place one tortilla on a microwave-safe plate, sprinkle cheese and fillings, top with second tortilla.
  2. Microwave 1–2 minutes until cheese melts. Slice and serve.

10-Minute Ramen Upgrade

1 serving • 10 minutes

Ingredients
  • 1 package instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • Handful of spinach
  • Soy sauce
  • Sriracha
Instructions
  1. Cook ramen as directed.
  2. In last 2 minutes, crack egg into pot and stir to create ribbons. Turn off heat and stir in spinach. Mix seasoning, soy sauce, and sriracha to taste.
