Overnight Oats

1 serving • 2 min prep (4+ hr chill)

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a jar.
  2. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
  3. Stir and enjoy cold.

Avocado Toast

1 serving • ~5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • Salt
  • Lemon (optional)
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Mash avocado with salt + lemon.
  3. Spread, drizzle with olive oil.
