Chicken Salad

2–3 servings • 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cooked Chicken
  • 1/3 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 stalk Celery, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Red Onion, finely chopped
  • Salt & Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, mayonnaise, celery, and red onion in a bowl.
  2. Mix until evenly combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper; chill or serve immediately.

Mozzarella Toast

1 serving • 5–10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread
  • 2-3 slices Fresh Mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp Pesto or drizzle Olive Oil
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Dried Italian Herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast bread, top with mozzarella and pesto/oil, season, and broil until cheese melts.
